COMPUTER BASED TEST FOR ADMISSION INTO HIGHER NATIONAL DIPLOMA PROGRAMMES FOR THE 2024/2025 ACADEMIC SESSION This is to inform all candidates who applied for admission into the Higher National Diploma (HND) Programmes of Auchi Polytechnic, Auchi, that the Online Computer Based Test (CBT) for admission for the 2024/2025 academic session will hold from
