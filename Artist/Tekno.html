Tekno

Tekno, whose real name is Augustine Miles Kelechi, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, producer, and entertainer. Here's a summary of his age, place of birth, and music career: - Age: Tekno is currently 32 years old, born on December 17, 1990. - Place of Birth: He was born in Bauchi State, Nigeria, but originally hails from Ivo Local Government Area in Ebonyi State. - Music Career: Tekno started singing at the age of 8 when he enrolled in a music school to learn piano and guitar. He began his professional music career in 2013 after signing a recording contract with Made Men Music Group (MMMG).
