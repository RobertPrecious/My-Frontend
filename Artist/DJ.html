Don Jazzy

Don Jazzy, whose real name is Michael Collins Ajereh, is a renowned Nigerian record producer, singer, and businessman. Here's a summary of his age, place of birth, and music career: - Age: Don Jazzy is currently 42 years old, born on November 26, 1982.¹ ² - Place of Birth: He was born in Umuahia, Abia State, Nigeria, but originally hails from Isoko, Delta State. - Music Career: Don Jazzy's music journey began at the age of 12 when he started playing the bass guitar and piano. He gained knowledge of traditional and percussion instruments, and his uncle invited him to play drums for a local church in London in 2000. He worked with various artists, including Solek, JJC Skillz, and D'Banj, before co-founding Mo' Hits Records with D'Banj in 2004. Don Jazzy is now the founder of Mavin Records, which he established in 2012.
