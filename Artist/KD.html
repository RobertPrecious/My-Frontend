Kizz Daniel

Kizz Daniel, whose real name is Anidugbe Daniel Oluwatobiloba, is a Nigerian singer and songwriter born on May 1, 1994, in Abeokuta, Ogun State.¹ ² He developed an interest in music at a remarkably young age - just seven years old. His father, recognizing his talent, encouraged and supported him, which helped shape his unique voice and rhythm. Kizz Daniel's music career officially took off in 2013 after he graduated with a degree in Water Resources Management and Agrometeorology from the Federal University of Agriculture in Abeokuta. He signed with G-Worldwide Entertainment and released his debut single, "Shoye," in May 2014.
