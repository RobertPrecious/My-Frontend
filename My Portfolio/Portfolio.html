About Me

Software Engineer!

Hello, I'm Uweh Reuben, a third-year student at theUniversity of Delta, Agbor, currently living in Delta State, Nigeria. My passion for technology began at an early age, sparked by a natural curiosity about how things work. I often found myself exploring gadgets, experimenting with software, and imagining how digital tools were built. Over time, this curiosity grew into a genuine desire to create and innovate using code. I officially began my programming journey in July 2024, and since then, I've been committed to learning the fundamentals of software development. I’m especially drawn to how software can solve real-life problems and improve everyday experiences. My ambition is to become a professional software engineer, using technology to build meaningful, user-focused solutions. I believe in approaching growth with discipline, consistency, and purpose. With every step forward, I’m focused on building not just skills, but a career that makes a positive impact through technology.
