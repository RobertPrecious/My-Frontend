Davido

Davido was born on November 21,1992,which makes him 33 years old(as of March 2023). He was born in Atlanta, Georgia,USA, but was raised in Lagos,Nigeria. Davido's interest in began at a young age.He started making music in his teenage years, influenced by an American rapper Lil Wayne . He attended Oakwood University in Alabama, USA, but dropped out to pursue a career in music. In 2010,Davido co-founded HKN Music, a record label with his cousin brother Adewale Adeleke. He released his debut single, "Back When",in 2011,but it was his second single "Dami Duro",that brought his mainstream success in Nigeria.Since then, Davido has become one of the most successful and influential musicians inn Africa and nationwide known for his hits like "fall", "if", "fem" and more.
