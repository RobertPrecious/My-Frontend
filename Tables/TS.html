Tiwa Savage

Tiwa Savage is actually a female artist, not male. Here's a summary of her age, place of birth, and music career: - Age: Tiwa Savage is currently 43 years old, born on February 5, 1980 [1]. - Place of Birth: She was born in Ikeja, Lagos State, Nigeria, but moved to London, England, at a young age [1]. - Music Career: Tiwa Savage started her music career at the age of 16, singing backup for artists like George Michael and Mary J. Blige. She later attended Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts, and graduated with a degree in Professional Music [1]. After working as a songwriter for artists like Babyface and Kat Deluna, Tiwa Savage moved back to Nigeria and signed with Mavin Records in 2012, releasing her debut single "Kele Kele Love" [1].
