Rema

Rema, whose real name is Divine Ikubor, is a Nigerian singer and rapper. Here's a summary of his age, place of birth, and music career: - Age: Rema is currently 22 years old, born on May 1, 2001. - Place of Birth: He was born in Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria. - Music Career: Rema started his music career at a young age, influenced by artists like 2face Idibia and 9ice. He gained popularity on social media platforms, particularly Instagram and Twitter, where he shared freestyle videos and gained the attention of music industry experts [1]. In 2019, Rema signed a record deal with Jonzing World and Mavin Records, and released his debut EP, "Rema," which included hits like "Dumebi" and "Iron Man".
