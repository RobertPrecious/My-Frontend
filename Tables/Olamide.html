Olamide

Olamide, whose real name is Olamide Gbenga Adedeji, is a Nigerian rapper, singer, songwriter, and record executive. Here's a summary of his age, place of birth, and music career: - Age: Olamide is currently 36 years old, born on March 15, 1989. - Place of Birth: He was born in Bariga, Lagos State, Nigeria. - Music Career: Olamide started his music career at the age of 21, releasing his debut single "Eni Duro" in 2010. He gained popularity with his debut album "Rapsodi" in 2011, which was released under Coded Tunes record label. Olamide later founded his own record label, YBNL Nation, in 2012, and has since released several successful albums.
