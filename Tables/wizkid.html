Wizkid

Wizkid, whose real name is Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun, was born on July 16, 1990, in the Ojuelegba suburb of Surulere, Lagos, Nigeria. He's currently 33 years old.Wizkid's music journey began at the age of 11 when he started recording music. He formed a group called the Glorious Five with his church friends, and they even released an album before disbanding. He later adopted the stage name Lil Prinz until 2006.
